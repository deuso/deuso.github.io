First Glimpse of Markdown

In coincidence, I get to know Markdown.
I believe it will be prevalent for everyday writing.

Markdown is the definitive writing style I have always been seeking for free writing, technical documentation recording and research paper composing.

Through a brief browsing, I find out a few ideas about future works related to markdown.

  • A translator between LaTeX and markdown, a *.md document can be transformed to *.tex format for rendering in a LaTeX system such as TexLive; Also, *.tex files can be interpreted by this translator to show tex documents in browsers directly.
  • An analyzer of *.pdf documents (eg. papers), which translate PDF files to markdown language to be shown and indexed in text format.
